# Step-by-Step Tutorial

## Step 1 — Customize Hero Section

- Open `sections/hero.html`
- Update your role/title if needed
- Replace the tagline with your own introduction

## Step 2 — Update About Section

- Open `sections/about.html`
- Write your personal story
- Update the skill cards to match your expertise

## Step 3 — Add Projects

- Open `sections/projects.html`
- Replace placeholder projects with your real work
- Add project images to `assets/` and update image paths

## Step 4 — Build Contact Form

- Open `sections/contact.html`
- Connect the form to a service like Formspree or Netlify Forms

## Step 5 — Deploy to Vercel

- Push your code to GitHub
- Import the repository on [vercel.com](https://vercel.com)
- Deploy with one click
