About Me

I’m a passionate Game Developer and creative storyteller currently focused on learning and mastering Unreal Engine and Blender to build immersive 3D environments, realistic assets, and engaging gameplay experiences. My journey in game development started from a deep love for video games, and today that passion drives me to create worlds, mechanics, and stories from scratch.


I am currently working on an indie horror game, where I explore gameplay design, level creation, scripting, and cinematic storytelling. Alongside development, I also write game scripts and narratives to make experiences feel more emotional, immersive, and memorable. For me, game development is more than just coding — it’s the excitement of turning ideas into playable realities and constantly improving them until they feel alive.


Every project teaches me something new, whether it’s designing environments in Blender, creating mechanics in Unreal Engine, or learning advanced workflows used in modern game production. I enjoy the process of learning, experimenting, failing, improving, and building better experiences every day — and I plan to keep creating and evolving as a developer.


Apart from game development, I’m also a passionate Web Developer skilled in technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other modern web development tools. I enjoy building responsive, interactive, and visually engaging websites that deliver smooth user experiences. I also use AI-powered tools to improve workflows, debug efficiently, and create modern solutions for clients and personal projects.


Whether I’m developing a horror game, designing a 3D environment, or crafting a responsive website, my goal is always the same — to create experiences that feel meaningful, polished, and impactful.
